History of Movies

The Beginning

The first talking movie ("talkie") was made in 1927. Before this movies were silent.

The first silent movie (film that tells a story) premiered in 1903. The motion picture camera was only 15 years old at the time, having been invented in 1887.

The Golden Age

The golden age of Hollywood lasted from the late 1920s to the early 1960s.
